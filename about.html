Tim grew up in Evanston, IL, just outside of Chicago. Attended DePaul University in Chicago to study Digital Cinema and Photography and graduated in 2010.

Following school Tim spent time in Chicago's flourishing and prominent restaurant and bar scene. He developing an appreciation for spirits, cocktails, and hospitality during his tenure at The Aviary where he expedited in the kitchen and later worked at The Publican.

Personal interests and hobbies include home brewing and photography. There's also nothing better than passing the time with friends over a few boardgames.

In 2016 he will attend Dev BootCamp in Chicago to begin a new course of focus towards computer programing.
